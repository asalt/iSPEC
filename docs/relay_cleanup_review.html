Behavioral Boundary

The split keeps the same safety posture: relay requests are staged unless mode=send, confirm=true, live sending is enabled, the source/target policy passes, and the concrete adapter succeeds. Receipts remain the source of truth for outcome reporting.

No new autonomous behavior was added here. This is integration hardening and reviewability work, not a scheduler or always-on decision loop.
